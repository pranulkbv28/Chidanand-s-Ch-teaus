About this space

Once upon a time in Bengaluru- a quaint, airy, private home took birth from the idea of travelling across countries & by collecting experiences we have liked over the years which we have curated for visitors and travelers alike. Located in a quiet neighborhood of Bengaluru, the home comfortably accommodates 2 people which is 30 mins away from the Airport and 40 mins away from the central hubs of the city. The place itself is at walking distances to many eateries, cafes, parks & pubs to unwind.

The space

Welcome to Bengaluru :)

Enjoy the serene atmosphere of Bengaluru with birds chirping in the evenings and trees around the home. You will be located on the third floor with a lot of potted flowering plants with privacy and a balcony.

Guest Access

There is a separate entrance to the home on the 3rd floor and with stairs to keep everyone fit.

Other things to note

Please note that we do not accept any check ins post 11 p.m.
